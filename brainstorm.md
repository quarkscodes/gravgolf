GravGolf will be 3d golf based game utilizing planet generation and gravity to make holes that require landing the ball on other planets

feature ideas:
-procedurally generated holes?
-create my own courses?
-player has different clubs with different angles and powers including putter, wedge, iron, driver/wood (how many different ones needed?)
-start with simple classic "mario golf" style golf swing where you set the aim and ideal power then have to time button presses to execute
-there is an estimated trajectory path that is traced with a 3d line to aid in shot setup
-player gets a golf cart with 6 axis control (or something more simplified)
-on some hits the player will have to attempt to hit the ball out of gravity's pull into the pull of another nearby planet object
-the actual holes will be standard red flag on a white pole standing in a white bordered hole
-player will get to ride around in golf cart to check out hole before starting

This game will ideally end up being multiplayer
multiplayer feature ideas:
-incorporate golf cart races with bumper-car style shenanigans
    -maybe before each hole instead of a free roam look-around there is a race and the winner gets some bonus on the hole
